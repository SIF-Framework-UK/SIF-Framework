Overview

The SIF Framework provides a robust system for ethical AI decision-making through structured debate and consensus-building among multiple AI models.

Innovative Approach: Utilizes competing AI ethical frameworks to reach balanced decisions through structured debate.

Multi-Model Debate

Consequentialist, deontological, and other AI models debate ethical queries

Immutable Ledger

All decisions and reasoning are recorded on a transparent ledger

Governance Framework

Constitutional principles guide the adjudication process
